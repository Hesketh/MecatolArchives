These fonts are for:
card names/subtitles/rulebook headings/unit abilities like BOMBARDMENT etc (SLIDER TI4, all caps)
the word ACTION: (Myriad pro bold italic)
card text triggers with a colon, eg “At the start of combat:” (Myriad pro semibold)
strategy card/game card body text and abilities (Myriad pro regular/semibold depending on taste)
flavour text (Myriad pro regular italic)
Box/rules cover art (remember not to say Twilight Imperium to remain in FFG fan content guidelines) (Ambroise Firmin Bold)
